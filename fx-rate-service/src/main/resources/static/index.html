fx-rate-service

Reference Data Ingestion CryptoFlow · EDPO FS2026

Polls a public FX provider on a timer, filters and translates the response, and publishes one FxRate per currency pair to the compacted topic reference.fx.rate. The compacted topic is the cache; downstream services materialise it as a GlobalKTable (see ADR-0029).
